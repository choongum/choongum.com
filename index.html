Choongum. Raised on San Francisco's experimental hip-hop and electronic scenes, Choongum's productions are hazy, swampy, and melodic. This signature sound defines his studio work and has taken his music to audiences internationally.

As a DJ, his selection is entirely mood-dependent. He moves easily from ambient and sunset progressive house to high-energy trance and noisy hardcore. Whatever the direction, he maintains a consistent atmosphere that makes every set feel like a direct extension of his own sound.
