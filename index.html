Bio

Raised on the outskirts of the San Francisco Bay Area, Choongum's productions channel the playful energy of Hyphy alongside the swampy, atmospheric textures of the city's 90s electronic underground.

As a DJ, his selection reflects these same moods, whether it's an extended chill-out room set or a frenetic club mix.

His unique productions have earned him SoundCloud's Artist of the Day and Bandcamp's New and Notable, while his selection has taken him across the globe, from the clubs of Mexico City to the jungles of Sri Lanka to the beaches of Australia.
